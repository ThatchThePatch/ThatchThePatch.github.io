About me

I’m Thatcher Pacholl, an undergraduate in Electrical Engineering at Oregon State University, graduating 2030. This portfolio documents my work, internships, and experiments.

I have a passion for the infrastructure that keeps the world connected, from power systems to the networks running on top of them. I'm also drawn to work that has real physical stakes, whether that's in a server room or out in the field.

My interests run from nuclear power systems down to the circuit level, I'm as drawn to the big questions in energy as I am to the hands-on work of designing and building hardware.
